Vegetarian Diet Plan

A simple and balanced daily nutrition guide

Morning

Start your day with water and light nutrition to activate digestion.

  • Warm water
  • Fruits or nuts

Breakfast

  • Oatmeal with milk or plant milk
  • Whole-grain toast
  • Fruit smoothie

Lunch

  • Whole grains (rice, quinoa, or bread)
  • Legumes or lentils
  • Vegetables (steamed or cooked)

Evening Snack

  • Nuts or seeds
  • Fruit or yogurt

Dinner

  • Light vegetables
  • Protein source (beans, tofu, or paneer)

Healthy eating supports a healthy lifestyle.
