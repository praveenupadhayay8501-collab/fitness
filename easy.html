EASY HOME WORKOUT

Easy Home Workouts are designed for beginners who want to stay active without going to the gym. These exercises are simple, safe, and require no equipment, making them perfect for students and busy people. You can do them in a small space at home and follow them at your own pace. The goal is to build strength, improve flexibility, and create a healthy daily habit without putting stress on your body.

1. Jumping Jacks


Stand straight

Jump legs out, hands up

Jump back to start


2. Wall Sit


Back against wall

Slide down like sitting

Hold position


3. Push-Ups


Hands under shoulders

Lower chest

Push back up


4. Abdominal Crunch


Lie on back

Knees bent

Lift shoulders slightly


5. Step-Up onto Chair


Step one foot on chair

Push body up

Step down, switch leg


6. Squats


Feet shoulder-width

Sit back and down

Stand up


7. Triceps Dip (Chair)


Hands on chair edge

Lower body

Push up


8. Plank


Forearms on floor

Body straight

Hold steady


9. High Knees (Running)


Run in place

Lift knees high

Fast pace


10. Lunges


Step one leg forward

Bend both knees

Return, switch leg


11. Push-Up & Rotation


Do one push-up

Rotate body

Raise one arm


12. Side Plank


Lie on side

Elbow under shoulder

Hold body straight
